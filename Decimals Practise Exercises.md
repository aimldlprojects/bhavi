### Comparision of decimal numbers

1. ₹5.50 vs ₹5.45
2. ₹2.75 vs ₹3.10
3. ₹7.89 vs ₹7.90
4. ₹0.99 vs ₹1.00
5. ₹10.05 vs ₹10.50

   **Answer:**

   1. ₹5.50 is greater.
   2. ₹3.10 is greater.
   3. ₹7.90 is greater.
   4. ₹1.00 is greater.
   5. ₹10.50 is greater.
